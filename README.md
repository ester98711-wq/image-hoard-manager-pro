## 🚀 Key Features

### 🧠 Intelligent Bulk Ingestion Engine
- **Smart URL Interpretation** – Parses complex gallery structures, lazy-loaded galleries, and infinite-scroll visual feeds across thousands of domains.
- **Multi-Threaded Extraction Pipeline** – Processes up to 500 simultaneous connections with adaptive throttling to respect server limits.
- **Format Harmony** – Automatically normalizes to WebP, AVIF, JPEG-XL, or PNG while preserving EXIF data and color profiles.

- **Nested Collection Trees** – Create multi-level folder structures with virtual symlinks for cross-referencing without duplication.

- **Provenance Stamping** – Every image batch receives a SHA-512 manifest signed with your personal key.

### ⚡ Performance That Scales
- **Adaptive Resource Governor** – Dynamically balances CPU, RAM, and disk I/O based on your system profile.
- **Batch Preview & Validation** – Review thumbnails, check for duplicates, and fix broken metadata before finalizing any operation.
- **Resume Capability** – Interrupted operations restart from the last successful item, not from zero.

## 📚 How It Works (The Philosophy)

Think of ImageVault Pro as a **digital conservator** rather than a simple tool. Where other software sees URLs, ImageVault Pro sees **threads of visual context**. It understands that an image is never just a file—it is a moment, a timestamp, a set of relationships with other images, and a piece of a larger narrative.

### The Three Pillars

1. **Capture** – Collect images from any source that respects standard web protocols. The engine mimics a legitimate browser session (complete with user-agent rotation) to ensure compatibility without deception.
2. **Curate** – Apply your organizational schema. Use the **Semantic Loom** to weave images together by content similarity, creation date, or custom rules.
3. **Conserve** – Store, backup, and share with integrity. Every export includes a manifest that can be verified independently.

This approach means that whether you are archiving 50,000 historical photographs from a university library or consolidating your personal travel albums, the result is the same: a pristine, authenticated, and deeply searchable archive.

## 🧩 Modules Included

- **Batch Matcher** – Deduplication engine that compares images by perceptual hash, not just filename or size.
